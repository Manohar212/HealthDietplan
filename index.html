HEALTHBUDDY DIET PLANS

Tailored Diets for Chronic Conditions - Stay Safe, Stay Healthy!

Diet Plan for Diabetes

Uses: Helps manage blood sugar levels, supports insulin function, and reduces risk of complications like neuropathy and heart issues.

Recommended Foods

  • Whole grains (brown rice, oats)
  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Non-starchy vegetables
  • Lean proteins (tofu, chicken)
  • Healthy fats (avocado, olive oil)

Avoid

  • Sugary drinks, refined carbs
  • Deep-fried and sweetened snacks

Diet Plan for Hypertension

Uses: Helps lower blood pressure, supports heart health, and reduces risk of stroke and kidney damage.

Recommended Foods

  • Bananas, oranges (rich in potassium)
  • Low-fat dairy products
  • Whole grains
  • Lean meats and fish
  • Unsalted nuts and seeds

Avoid

  • High sodium foods
  • Canned soups and processed meats

Diet Plan for Kidney Disease

Uses: Supports kidney function, controls fluid retention, and reduces waste buildup in the blood.

Recommended Foods

  • White rice, white bread (low potassium)
  • Apples, berries
  • Cauliflower, cabbage
  • Egg whites

Avoid

  • High potassium and phosphorus foods
  • Bananas, oranges, nuts

Diet Plan for Thyroid

Uses: Regulates hormone production, boosts metabolism, and supports energy levels and mood.

Recommended Foods

  • Iodine-rich foods (iodized salt, seafood)
  • Brazil nuts (selenium)
  • Eggs, dairy
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables

Avoid

  • Excess soy products
  • Cruciferous vegetables (raw cabbage, broccoli in excess)

Diet Plan for Liver Disease

Uses: Detoxifies the liver, reduces fat accumulation, and improves enzyme levels and liver function.

Recommended Foods

  • High-fiber foods (fruits, whole grains)
  • Green tea
  • Cruciferous vegetables
  • Lean proteins

Avoid

  • Alcohol, fried foods
  • Sugar-rich snacks and refined carbs

Diet Plan for Cancer

Uses: Strengthens immunity, maintains energy levels, and supports body repair during/after treatment.

Recommended Foods

  • Colorful fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Legumes and nuts
  • Lean meat or plant-based proteins

Avoid

  • Processed meats, sugary foods
  • High-sodium packaged snacks
